Before Your Visit

Get the most from
your telehealth visit.

A few minutes of preparation makes for a faster, more focused appointment — so you leave with everything you need.

💻 Technology Check — What You Need

📸
Camera & Mic Built into any modern smartphone, tablet, or laptop
🌎
Internet Connection WiFi or 4G/5G — a stable connection prevents freezing
🔍
Modern Browser Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge — no app to download
🔒
Private Space A quiet, well-lit location where you can speak freely
💌
Secure Link You'll receive a visit link via email before your appointment
⏱
5 Min Early Click your link a few minutes early to test your audio & video
📋

Health Information to Have Ready

  • List of all current medications (name, dose, frequency)
  • List of any vitamins, supplements, or over-the-counter drugs
  • Known allergies (medication and other)
  • Description of your symptoms — when they started, how severe, what makes them better or worse
  • Any recent lab results or test reports you'd like reviewed
  • Family medical history (if relevant to your concern)
  • Name of your preferred pharmacy (and its address)
🏫

For Specific Visit Types

  • Chronic conditions: Recent blood pressure readings, blood sugar log, or weight log if monitoring those
  • DOT Physical: Your CDL license or driver's license, and a list of any health conditions or medications you take
  • Sports Physical: Your school's or league's required form — we'll sign and return it
  • Weight loss: Your current weight, height, and any prior weight loss attempts
  • Lab review: Upload or have the lab report open on your device to share during the visit
  • Skin concern: Have good lighting so I can clearly see the area on camera
What to Expect

How Your Visit Flows

Here's what happens from the moment you book to the moment you're done — typically 20–30 minutes total.

1

Booking Confirmation (immediate)

After booking you'll receive a confirmation email with your appointment time, a secure visit link, and a brief intake form to complete. The intake form takes about 3–5 minutes and covers your reason for visiting, medications, and allergies.

2

Before the Visit (15 min prior)

Find a quiet, private space with good lighting. Test your camera and microphone by clicking your visit link early — it'll show a preview before entering the room. Have your medication list nearby.

3

The Visit (15–25 minutes)

I'll review your intake and health history, discuss your concerns in detail, and complete any relevant examination via video. I'll explain my findings and walk you through your care plan. There's always time for your questions — please ask them.

4

After the Visit (same day)

Any prescriptions are sent electronically to your pharmacy — usually ready within 1–2 hours. If labs or imaging were ordered, you'll receive electronic orders to take to your nearest LabCorp or Quest. You'll also receive a visit summary with your care plan and any follow-up instructions.

💡 Quick Tips for the Best Experience

  • Charge your device fully before the visit
  • Use WiFi over cellular when possible for a more stable connection
  • Close other apps and browser tabs to free up bandwidth
  • Use earbuds or headphones to improve audio clarity
  • Sit with light in front of you (facing a window) rather than behind you
  • Wear comfortable clothing you can adjust if showing a rash or injury
  • Write down your questions in advance — it's easy to forget during the visit
  • Have a notepad handy to write down care instructions
